# About me [EN]
MSc Candidate in Chemical Engineering (Unicamp) and Chemical Engineer (UTFPR) with experience in R&D, process engineering, and process simulation. I completed an internship at Petrobras’ Paulínia Refinery (REPLAN), supporting process optimization and industrial project development. My research and professional experience focus on renewable chemicals and biofuels from biomass, with practical experience in physicochemical analysis, sustainable process development, and process simulation using Aspen Plus.
